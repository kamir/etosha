QnA [Questions & Answers]

Definitive answers and facts from Cloudera's internal mailing lists, intsurctor's guides, Skype chats, and blogs are collected. The system guides us through cleaning and obfuscuation of customer and employee names. Finally, both steps enable knowledge sharing with training partners.

Search in INTERNAL data »
All raw data is stored in HBase and indexed in SOLR. This is the first tier of a layerd knowledge repository. All the content of this lowest layer is for internal use and for analysis only.

Collected raw data and the index sit in the Cloudera training-cluster. Feel free to contribute new ideas for further analysis and information extraction! Start now with your JIRA .

Search in PUBLIC data »
The public data is accessibe in a second tier. An offline reader enables all Cloudera trainers to use the knowledge base also in critical offline situations. VPN users can asses this data also via HUE.

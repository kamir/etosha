EDC: The Etosha Dataset Catalog

We collect and connect information about datasets. Whatever we find in the WWW or diagnostics tools provide, we put into the context. Context pools reside in a data-center or as part of an individual IT-system. Context pools can be connected. Based on trusted relationships we connect knowledge-clusters across the world.

For now, we provide a context pool master on our local servers until the founding allows us to run a permanent dataset-context-cloud on Amazon AWS or Google.
